## FAQ Section

### Headline
**Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: Do I really not need to create an account?**
A: Really! No account, no email, no password. Just open and use. Your data stays in your browser's local storage.

**Q: Is my data really private?**
A: Yes. Everything is stored locally on YOUR device. We literally can't access it‚Äîit never leaves your browser. No cloud sync, no data collection, no tracking.

**Q: What if I clear my browser data?**
A: Your tasks will be deleted (they're stored in localStorage). We're working on an export feature so you can back up your data.

**Q: Can I use it on multiple devices?**
A: Currently, VibeKanban is local-only, so each device has its own board. We're considering an optional (opt-in, privacy-conscious) cloud sync feature.

**Q: Is it really free forever?**
A: Yes. No trials, no "freemium" bait-and-switch. Core features are free forever. If we add premium features later, they'll be clearly optional.

**Q: Can I self-host it?**
A: Absolutely! VibeKanban is open source. Clone the repo, deploy to Vercel/Netlify/anywhere, and you're good to go.

**Q: Does it work on mobile?**
A: Yes! The responsive design works on phones and tablets. We're also planning native mobile apps.

**Q: Can I collaborate with my team?**
A: Not yet. VibeKanban is currently designed for individual use. Team features may come in the future based on user demand.

**Q: How do I contribute to the project?**
A: We'd love your help! Check out the GitHub repo for "good first issues" and contributing guidelines.

**Q: What happens if you shut down?**
A: Because it's local-first and open source, VibeKanban works independently of us. Clone the repo, self-host it, and it's yours forever.
