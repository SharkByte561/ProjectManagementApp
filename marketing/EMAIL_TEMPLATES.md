## Email Best Practices

### Sending Schedule

**Welcome sequence:**
- Day 0: Welcome email (immediate)
- Day 3: Tips & tricks email
- Day 7: Feature highlight email
- Day 14: Survey / feedback request

**Ongoing newsletter:**
- Frequency: Weekly (same day/time)
- Best days: Tuesday or Wednesday
- Best time: 10 AM in recipient's timezone

**Announcement emails:**
- Send immediately when launching new features
- Follow up with newsletter mention

### Technical Details

**From Name:** VibeKanban Team (or founder's name for personal touch)

**From Email:** hello@vibekanban.com (or similar)

**Reply-To:** Same as from email (and actually read/respond!)

**Unsubscribe:** One-click, no questions asked

**Privacy Note:** Add this to footer:
"We respect your privacy. We'll never sell your email. Unsubscribe anytime."

### Email Design Guidelines

**Keep it simple:**
- Plain text is fine (actually preferred by many)
- Minimal HTML if you must
- No heavy images (fast loading)
- Mobile-responsive

**Structure:**
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use separators (â”â”â”) for visual breaks
- Emoji sparingly (adds personality without overdoing it)
- Clear CTAs (one primary action per email)

**Tone:**
- Friendly, not corporate
- Honest, not salesy
- Helpful, not pushy
- Human, not robotic

### A/B Testing Ideas

Test these elements:
- Subject line length (short vs detailed)
- Emoji in subject line (yes vs no)
- Send time (morning vs afternoon)
- CTA wording ("Try it now" vs "Get started" vs "Check it out")
- Email length (short vs detailed)
- Personal vs company voice

### Privacy-First Email Marketing

**Respect users:**
- Explicit opt-in only (no pre-checked boxes)
- Easy unsubscribe (one click, no login required)
- No tracking pixels (or disclose them)
- No third-party data sharing
- Frequency control (let users choose)

**Be transparent:**
- Explain why you're emailing
- Show what data you have
- Allow data export/deletion
- No dark patterns

This aligns with VibeKanban's privacy-first philosophy.
